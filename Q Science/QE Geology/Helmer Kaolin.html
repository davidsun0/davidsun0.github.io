Helmer Kaolin

Chemistry

Physical Properties

Helmer Kaolin contains some larger particles. Helmer is not particularly plastic.

Helmer has a high iron content. It flashes a dark red in soda and wood firings. The iron is not particulate so Helmer does not have iron speckling in reduction.
