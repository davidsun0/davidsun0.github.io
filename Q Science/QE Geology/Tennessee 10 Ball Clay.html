Tennessee #10 Ball Clay

Tennessee #10 is a Ball Clay. Unlike other Kentucky and Tennessee ball clays, #10 has much lower silica content, yet still has high plasticity. Thus it is useful in making High Alumina Clay.

Chemistry

Tennessee #10 has significantly less silica content compared to other ball clays, yet it is still extremely plastic. I find Tennessee #10 extremely useful in making white stoneware bodies for soda firing. These clay bodies need plasticity but not silica; typically the more plastic a clay is the more silica content it has.

Physical Properties

Tennessee #10 is extremely plastic, like other ball clays.
