Laponite

Laponite is a synthetic Bentonite. Its particle sizes are much smaller than natural bentonites, which translates to thicker flow. Just 2% can gel water!

Rheological Additive for Cosmetic Applications - LAPONITE

Pottery suppliers do not supply Laponite, but it can be found at archiving material suppliers.

There are several different grades of Laponite which have differing properties.

Chemistry

Chemical formula: H12Li2Mg16Na2O72Si24

Oxides: Li2O Na2O 16MgO 24SiO2 6H2O

Oxide Weight Percent
SiO2 77.34
Li2O 0.8
Na2O 1.66
MgO 17.3
LOI 2.9

Rheology

  • Do not use Soda Ash, Sodium Silicate, Magnesium Sulfate, etc. to adjust slurries with Laponite. Laponite responds to pH (and is basic when mixed in water). Add acid to thicken.
