Tonbo

Tonbo is the Japanese word for dragonfly. Dragonflies are kind of cool.

  • The potter's dragonfly-shaped tool that measures both diameter and depth is called a tonbo.
    • It is used to produce items of the same size when Throwing off the Hump
  • Tonbogaeri (lit. dragonfly reverse) refers to how dragonflies can turn around in place
    • The Pokemon move Tonbogaeri is localized as U-Turn (which is why the move is bug-type)
    • Trains reversing out of a terminal are said to tonbogaeri
    • Ninja somersaulting is called tonbogaeri
