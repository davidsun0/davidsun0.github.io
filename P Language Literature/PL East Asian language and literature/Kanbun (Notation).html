Kanbun

This article is about the Japanese notation method for translating Chinese texts. In some contexts, the same word simply refers to Chinese texts.

Functions of kanbun:

  • Preserves the original classical Chinese text
  • Half translates the text into classical Japanese (some work is required by the reader)
