Fuyou Yuuna ha Yuusha denai - Fuyou Yuuna ha Kataribe tonaru Butaitanbou Report

The Butaitanbou Report was a promotional blog for Fuyou Yuna is not a Hero. The blog is about visiting various areas in Shikoku related to Yuki Yuna is a Hero.

This is an example of anime pilgrimage, not to be confused with the Shikoku Pilgrimage.
