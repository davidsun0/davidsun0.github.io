Confucius says...

  • 学而时习之，不亦说乎
  • 見義不為無勇也 (見義勇為)
    • To see (the opportunity to do) good and to not act is not courage

Notes

  • Traditionally the verb used in the phrase "Confucius says" is 曰, which is easily confused with the character for the sun: 日. That annoyed me at first, but I'm over it now.
