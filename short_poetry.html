short poetry

David Sun
2016 October 3
Medium: Library word magnets

Transcription

 Pathetic mindless villain need drama

inspire human escape

inspire trek for hard Destination travel everywhere search light

remember one good night desire dream in the trees

Wanted another fortress But different dungeon

never rest

will search never end

my hero battle s dark soulless figure

how can she Help me

We wander

I drink

you fight

I said why force every thing

trust answer watch when heart glow

beneath human flesh Grand romance outrun grave
