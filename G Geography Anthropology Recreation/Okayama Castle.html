Okayama Castle

Museum

The original castle no longer exists, but a modern replica is built on the site. The inside of the castle is a museum about Okayama history.

I highly recommend the museum only if you are relatively fluent in Japanese and interested in Okayama history

  • The artifacts while cool, are somewhat limited
    • If any samurai armor / sword is good enough for you, you might as well just go to the Tokyo Museum instead of going all the way out to Okayama
  • The museum is not very foreign language friendly
  • Much of the exhibit is focused on local history
    • And how people from Okayama were involved in the Battle of Sekigahara
