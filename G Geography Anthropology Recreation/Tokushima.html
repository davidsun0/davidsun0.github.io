Tokushima Prefecture

Tokushima is located on the eastern side of Shikoku. Tokushima Prefecture was formerly Awa Province.

  • Tokushima's Naruto Whirlpools is one of its most famous tourist attraction
  • Awa Odori is one of Japan's three big festival dances
  • The citrus Sudachi is local to Tokushima
  • Tokushima produces indigo dye and dyed fabric
    • Large Otani Ware pots are used for the dyeing process
