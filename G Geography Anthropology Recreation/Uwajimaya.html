Uwajimaya

Uwajimaya is a Japanese supermarket chain in the Pacific Northwest of the United States.

Uwajimaya was founded by Fujimatsu and Sadako Moriguchi in 1928. It is named after Uwajima City in Ehime, where Fujimatsu worked.
