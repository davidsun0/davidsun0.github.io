Murakami Kanamoto-ten

Murakami Kanamoto-ten (村上金物店) is a Pottery supply store in Seto, Aichi, Japan. They have a very wide range of pottery tools and ceramic raw materials (including rare stuff too!) A must visit if you're a potter and in the area.

  • Website: https://ceramictools.shop/
  • Instagram: @murakamikanamonoten
  • Address: 〒４８９－０８１７ 愛知県瀬戸市銀杏木町５６
    • 56 Ichounoki, Seto, Aichi 489-0817
    • Directions from Nagoya Station (takes 1 hour)
      • Ride the Higashiyama Line for Fujigaoka to Sakae Station
      • Transfer to the Seto Line for Owari-Seto
      • Walk 5 minutes south from Owari-Seto Station
