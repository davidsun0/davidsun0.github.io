Okayama

晴れの国岡山

Culture

  • Okayama is home to Bizen Ware, one of the Six Ancient Kiln Sites of Japan
  • Okayama Castle
  • The official Okayama Youtube Channel released Dameyo Manbiki Nante, an anti-theft PSA in 2015
    • It became an internet meme for its less than stellar animation quality and not very original character designs
  • The folktale hero Momotaro is from Okayama
    • In the story, Momotaro feeds wild animals kibidango, a local food
      • Kibidango are like mochi, chewy and mildly sweet
    • Momotaro travels to the neighboring Kagawa to defeat the monsters living there

Notes

  • The Okayama City Tram is the jankiest form of transportation I've ridden in Japan (so far)
    • Yet still better than Boston's Green Line
