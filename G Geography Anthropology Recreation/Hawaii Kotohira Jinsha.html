Kotohira Jinsha (Hawai'i)

Kotohira Jinsha is a Shinto shrine in Honolulu. It shares a site with the Hawaii Dazaifu Tenmagu.

This shrine takes its name from Kotohira Jinja in Kotohira, Kagawa.

Kotohira Jinsha is cool because of its American influences.

  • It sells omamori like shrines in Japan, but they're Hawai'i and American themed
    • There are USA flag themed omamori
    • And ones with 808 (Hawaii's telephone area code) designs
