Marugame

Marugame is a city in Kagawa.

  • Marugame Seimen is an Sanuki Udon restaurant chain with locations across Japan.
    • Apparently the company has nothing to do with the city except for the name
  • Marugame is the setting of Nogi Wakaba is a Hero.
  • Hassaku is a festival unique to Marugame and western Kagawa.
