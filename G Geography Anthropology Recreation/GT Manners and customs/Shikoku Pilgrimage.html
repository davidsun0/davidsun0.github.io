Shikoku Pilrimage

The Shikoku Pilgrimage (遍路 henro) visits 88 shrines across Shikoku.

  • There is a modern pilgrimage based off of the Shikoku one that visits 88 spiritual locations in Nagasaki City.
  • In 2014, there were anti-Korean racist signs posted at the shrines.
    • Sara Kang's Contested Pilgrimage: Shikoku Henro and Dark Tourism essay talks about the incident and the nationalist backgrounds of the pilgrimage.
