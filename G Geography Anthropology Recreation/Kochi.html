Kochi

Kochi is the prefecture in Shikoku that I have the least to talk about. Kochi spans the southern side of Shikoku. The capital and most populous city is Kochi City.

  • Gomen Station is a fun station to take a picture of yourself dogeza-ing at because the name is a homophone for "sorry".
  • Tosashimizu, Kochi is the place in Japan furthest from Tokyo by travel time

Yusuhara

Yusuhara, Kochi, is a small town of a few thousand located in the mountains and has a library designed by fucking Kengo Kuma. It's absolutely gorgeous and has no right being in such a remote location.
