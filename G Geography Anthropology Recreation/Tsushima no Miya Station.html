Tsushima no Miya Station

Tsushima no Miya Station is a train station on the Yosan Line in Toyama, Kagawa. It only operates two days a year for the Tsushima no Miya festival on August 4th and 5th. This makes it the station with the fewest operating days in all of Japan.
