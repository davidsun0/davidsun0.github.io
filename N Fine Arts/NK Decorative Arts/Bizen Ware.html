Bizen Ware

Bizen ware (bizen yaki), one of the Six Ancient Kiln Sites of Japan, is pottery made in Bizen (in modern day Okayama).

  • Bizen ware
    • is unglazed, vitrified, high temp (~1300 C) stoneware
    • is unique for its Hidasuki, streaks of bright red and orange
    • forms natural ash glaze from exposure to the wood ash

A Bizen-ware kiln in Imbe, Okayama. Picture taken April 2023.
